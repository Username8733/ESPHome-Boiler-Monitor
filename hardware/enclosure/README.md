# Enclosure STL export

Generated from the two OpenSCAD files currently in the public repository.

## Files

- `hardware/enclosure/stl/boiler_alert_case.stl`
- `hardware/enclosure/stl/boiler_monitor_case.stl`

Each STL contains:

- Base enclosure: 100 × 60 × 30 mm
- Wall thickness: 2 mm
- Separate lid positioned 10 mm above the base in Y, matching the SCAD layout
- Lid holes:
  - Red LED: 5 mm
  - Green LED: 5 mm
  - Buzzer: 12 mm
  - Mute button: 7 mm

Triangle counts:

- boiler_alert_case.stl: 23748
- boiler_monitor_case.stl: 23748

The two SCAD files are geometrically identical except for comments.
